Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Superthoughts ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it.

What we collect

  • Email address and first name when you sign up for a complimentary session or purchase a program.
  • Payment information processed securely through Stripe. We never see or store your full card number.
  • Usage data such as pages visited and session progress, stored locally on your device via localStorage.

How we use your information

  • To deliver the content you requested (sessions, programs).
  • To send occasional emails about new programs or updates. You can unsubscribe at any time.
  • To improve our website and content based on aggregate usage patterns.

Third-party services

We use the following services:

  • Stripe for payment processing.
  • Kit (ConvertKit) for email delivery.
  • Bunny CDN for audio file delivery.
  • GitHub Pages for website hosting.

Each of these services has its own privacy policy. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Cookies and local storage

We use localStorage to remember your session progress and email access. We do not use tracking cookies or advertising pixels.

Data retention

We retain your email address for as long as you remain subscribed to our mailing list. You can request deletion at any time by emailing us.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time. Contact us at hello@superthoughts.com.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, email us at hello@superthoughts.com.
